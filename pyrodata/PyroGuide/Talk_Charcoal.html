[edit] Agrarian Airfloat

Two 20l oil drums.Large hole in top of one ,smaller hole in bottom of other. Fill drum 1 with dead (longdead best,not rotten) willow hand sawn to length.Make fire ,place drum on fire,cover with drum 2,leave cap on drum 2 with 5mm hole drilled in it.Build up fire round drum 1. No leaks xcept cap .Cook 2hrs or till smoke stops.Odd kick and shake helps. Beware burns,air gas mixtures. Remove from heat.Cool overnight. Place product into 20l plastic bucket ,pound with fat end hoe handle like african milling millet.Lung danger. Rotate handle as well as pound.Mix too. Two bowls,range of seives starting with rain tank leaf strainer,colander then flour seive. Big mixing bowls. Seive from bowl to bowl carefully as it loses big particles it behaves like a liquid .Use mask.Return chunks to bucket. Regrind. Not too much grinding ,if it resists we dont want it. Yeilds about 300g airfloat.Almost fills 5l container.Bigger particles retained for other purposes.Enough for 2kilos quality b.p..
