[edit] Vinsol Resin

Vinsol resin, while not common, is used as a binder, especially in blackpowder rockets. It is extracted as a byproduct from pine trees. Solvents include alcohol, and hydrocarbons (acetone, etc.) Vinsol resin can be purchased from chemical distributors and online pyrotechnic supply stores.
